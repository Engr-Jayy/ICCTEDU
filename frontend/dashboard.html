Help & Support Center

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who can use this EduZone?
1. Professors per department are authorized to book rooms.

2. The School Administrator shall forward the reserved room request to the Vice President.

3. The Vice President oversees reserved room authorization.

How do I login?

Users can access EduZone using CD Key.

How do I reserve a classroom?
1. Login as professor.

2. Go to "Reservation Manager" and pick a room.

3. Select date, time and room.

4. Submit reservation request.

Note: Booking a room reservations are ONLY available within 06:00AM - 03:00PM. Reservations past working hours will not be approved.

How long does scheduling a room take to be approved?

Request are usually reviewed within 06:00AM - 03:00PM. Status updates can be checked inside "Request Status" tab.

How do I know if the room is already booked?

The selected room will display the specific time as “Booked.”

Can I book a room in advance?

It allows recurring reservations in advance if rooms are available.

Can I see past reservations?

Only the School Admin and the Vice President have access to the reserved room history.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Currently, cancellations can be done via the "Request Status" tab or by contacting the administrator.

Does my reservation need an approval?

Reservations are reviewed by the School Admin first, then forwarded to the Vice President for authorization.

What happens if my reservation is rejected?

You will be notified via "Request Status" to see if the reserved room is approved or denied.

Who do I contact for support?

Other concerns can be submitted through "Help / Support" inside box.

Still need help?

If your problem isn't listed above, please send us an email and our team will get back to you.
